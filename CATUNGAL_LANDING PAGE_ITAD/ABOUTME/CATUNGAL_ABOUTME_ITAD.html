Everlasting Memories

Childhood Years

           I am Mark Angelo Catungal from Marikina City. 1 Childhood is the most fun and memorable time in anyone’s life. 2 It is the first stage of my life which we enjoy in whatever way we like. 3 Besides, this is the time that shapes up our future. 4 The memories of my childhood ultimately become the lifelong memory which always brings a smile on my face. 5 As we grow, we feel more attachment to our childhood, and we want to have a memory lane on that period. 6 Everyone has a few memories from their childhood that seem so vividly true that we cling onto them and believe that every single little detail of this memory is completely true. 7 Growing up, I had a very loving family. Since I am the second to the eldest it is just my brother who I used to play every day. 8 One day when I was 3 or 4 years old, we have a yearly vacation on my father’s province. 9 Every year we always went to Bonoan Beach in Pangasinan for our swimming bonding with cousins’ uncles, aunties, and my grandmother. 10 Because I was young, I enjoyed playing in sand and making a hole and sometimes a little castle. 11 I enjoyed playing that I forgot which cottage we rented because all the cottages look just the same. 12 I keep on walking and cried hard because I cannot find our cottage. 13 I thought I cannot find my family but fortunately 3 native teenagers asked and accompanied me to look for our cottage and my parents. 14 After some time, they finally found my father who at that time also keep on looking. 15 He immediately approached the 3 boys and Thank them.16I will not be forgot that experience because I am still that young, but I experienced that nerve shocking experiences.17 On this time also when I experienced stepping on a rusted nail that resulted for my jaw locking.18 My mom and dad brought me to Barangay Sta Elena so that I will be treated and vaccinated by anti-tetanus. 19 They found a doctor but there is no available medicine. 20 He went to many branches of Mercury drug it is out of stack. 21 My mother told my dad to go back to Barangay Parang and had my anti tetanus shot at Parang Health Center. 22 There, I was shot by an anti-tetanus and was given a medicine that I will take for 2 days. 23
